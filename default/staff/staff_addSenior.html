Applicant Agreement

By signing this agreement, the applicant acknowledges and agrees to the terms outlined herein regarding the housing project lot allocation. The applicant understands that upon approval of the housing project, they are required to demolish their existing house located on the designated lot. This demolition must be completed before the applicant can occupy the new housing project lot. The applicant is responsible for all costs associated with the demolition process, including obtaining necessary permits and ensuring compliance with local regulations. Furthermore, the applicant agrees to vacate the existing property in a timely manner to facilitate the project’s schedule. Failure to comply with this requirement may result in delays or forfeiture of the housing project lot. The applicant acknowledges that the organization is not liable for any damages or losses incurred during the demolition process. Additionally, the applicant must provide proof of demolition to the housing authority prior to moving into the new housing project lot. By signing this agreement, the applicant confirms their understanding and acceptance of these terms.
